# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Aquaglow.co.in Website
**Prepared by:** Blue Panda
**Client:** Aquaglow Enterprises
**Brand:** Dorron
**Date:** April 2026
## 1. Executive Summary
This PRD outlines the strategy, design, and technical requirements for the new website of Aquaglow Enterprises, specifically focusing on its flagship cleaning brand, **Dorron**. The objective is to design a sleek, modern digital storefront that bridges the gap between B2C consumer appeal and B2B distributor conversion. The site must encapsulate the brand's vision for 2026-2031: *"Clean Homes. Brighter Futures. Dorron."*
## 2. Strategic Positioning & Target Audience
The core challenge and opportunity of this platform is serving two distinct audiences simultaneously without diluting the message.
 * **End Users (Consumers):** Driven by emotion, visual trust, and everyday utility. They need to see a reliable, safe, and effective "Mid-tier modern FMCG brand."
 * **Distributors/Wholesalers (B2B):** Driven by logic, margins, and market demand. They need to see a highly professional, growing brand that is easy to partner with.
**The Golden Rule for the UI:** The site must look like it is built for consumers but convert like it is built for distributors.
## 3. Visual & Copy Direction
### 3.1 Design Aesthetic
 * **Vibe:** Bright, clean, and functional.
 * **Color Palette:** Predominantly white backgrounds with slight, targeted color accents that match the specific product being highlighted (e.g., blue for glass cleaner, green for floor cleaner).
 * **Imagery Strategy:** A deliberate mix of AI-generated lifestyle visuals (to evoke emotion and aspiration) and high-quality product packaging shots (to build tangible trust).
### 3.2 Copy & Tone
 * **Tone:** Simple, clear, functional, and slightly aspirational.
 * **Guidelines:** Absolutely no corporate jargon. Avoid phrases like "Revolutionary cleaning solutions" or "Cutting-edge technology."
 * **Approved Phrasing:** "Removes tough stains easily," "Designed for everyday use," "Reliable performance."
## 4. Site Architecture & Page Requirements
### 4.1 Homepage (Conversion-First Focus)
**Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)**
 * **Goal:** Instantly establish legitimacy as a modern FMCG brand.
 * **Headline:** *“Powerful Cleaning. Trusted Everyday.”*
 * **Subtext:** *“Dorron by Aquaglow — effective, affordable, and made for modern homes.”*
 * **CTAs:** Primary: "Explore Products" | Secondary: "Become a Distributor"
**Section 2: Product Categories (Visual Grid)**
 * **Layout:** Highly skimmable grid highlighting the core verticals (Dishwash, Toilet Cleaner, Floor Cleaner, Glass Cleaner, Detergent, Tile Cleaner).
 * **Card Design:** High-res product image + one-liner benefit (e.g., *“Tough on grease, gentle on hands”*).
**Section 3: Trust Builder ("Why Dorron?")**
 * **Content Pillars:** Effective cleaning performance, Value for money, Safe for daily use, Consistent quality.
**Section 4: The Distributor Pitch**
 * **Headline:** *“Partner With a Growing Cleaning Brand”*
 * **Key Selling Points:** High demand FMCG category, Attractive margins, Consistent supply chain.
 * **Action:** Direct link to the distributor onboarding funnel.
### 4.2 Product Catalog Page
A clean, grid-based catalog showcasing the active SKUs. The interface should be easy for wholesalers to review pack sizes and MRPs.
**Inventory Integration Requirements:**
 * **Floor Cleaners:** White Floor Cleaner PH (1L - ₹99, 5L - ₹321), Dorron Floor Cleaner Advance (1L - ₹199, 5L - ₹799)
 * **Dishwash:** Dorron Dishwash Clean (1L Pouch - ₹199, 5L - ₹799)
 * **Toilet Care:** Dorron Toilet Cleaner (500ml - ₹79, 1L - ₹199, 5L - ₹1199)
 * **Glass Cleaners:** Dorron Glass Cleaner (500ml - ₹81, 5L - ₹799)
 * **Laundry/Detergent:** Dorron Liquid Detergent (1L Pouch - ₹199, 1L - ₹199, 5L - ₹999)
 * **Specialty:** Dorron Tile Cleaner (1L - ₹129, 5L - ₹799), Dorron Handcleanser (5L - ₹799)
### 4.3 About Us (Company Profile & Leadership)
This page will humanize the brand by leveraging the founder's extensive background.
 * **The Aquaglow Story:** Highlight the 3-year journey of Aquaglow and the introduction of the Dorron brand.
 * **Leadership Bio:** Feature the founder's 12 years of deep-rooted experience spanning sales, marketing, trading, and manufacturing, emphasizing the transition from a 5-year corporate career to industry entrepreneurship.
 * **Future Vision (2026-2031):** Detail the commitment to innovation, sustainability, and the mission to make Dorron a nationally recognized household name.
### 4.4 Distributor & Contact Portal
 * **Functionality:** A streamlined B2B inquiry form capturing essential data (Name, Region, Expected Volume, Current Distribution Network).
 * **Trust Elements:** Basic contact info, company headquarters location, and a clear outline of wholesale terms (e.g., GST exclusivity, authorized distributorship rules).
## 5. Technical Specifications
 * **Responsive Architecture:** The UI must be flawlessly adaptive, ensuring the visual grids and product catalogs render perfectly on mobile devices, which is critical for local distributors operating on the go.
 * **Performance Optimization:** As an FMCG site, image loading speeds must be optimized. Lifestyle images and product renders should be served in modern, compressed formats (WebP/AVIF).
 * **Lead Routing Automation:** The B2B distributor form should securely route inquiries directly to the Aquaglow sales team, potentially integrating with a lightweight CRM or automated notification system to ensure zero drop-off for high-value leads.
 * **Deployment Environment:** Designed to be hosted on a fast, scalable infrastructure (e.g., Netlify or Google Cloud Run) ensuring high uptime and reliable performance during marketing pushes.
